Welcome to Sonora Shores!

Sonora Shores is a twelve-unit development residing on beautiful Lake Osoyoos, located in Oroville, WA. These water front properties allow you to relax on twenty-two kilometers of luxurious beaches and fish the warmest waters in Canada. Explore the countryside or treat yourself on the wine trail, Sonora Shores offers the ultimate guest experience for everyone.

Oroville, WA
Quiet and spectacular

Oroville, WA, the quaint community just four miles south of the Canadian border, is a dream location for vacationers and retirees alike. The green pastures, orchards and vineyards that surround the city conjure up images of the Italian countryside. Constrast with the ocassional rugged cliff makes the landscape truly special.

The city is pockmarked with wineries, craft breweries, restaurants & parks. With a population of just 1700 the city really feels like a safe and pleasant place to live.
