Osoyoos Lake

Osoyoos lake is one of Canada's largest and most beautiful. The excellent fishing, boating and surrounding geography make it one of the worlds most popular tourist destinations. Not only does the lake boast the title of warmest freshwater lake in Canada, osoyoos is also bordered by kilometers of gorgeous beaches and parks that are gauranteed to make vacations a treat.

Tourism

Tourism is the largest industry in the Osoyoos area.
