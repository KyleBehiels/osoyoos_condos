About Us

Sonora Shores is a father son development located in Oroville, WA. Jason and his father, Gordon, built these condos with the goal of creating the best possible vacation destination

The location was purposefully chosen in order to be as quiet, humble and enjoyable as possible. Gordon and Jason really set out to create an environment where you can get away from the tedium and stress of everyday life and relax for months at a time. Their commitment to this development is apparent in both its current progress and their plans for its future.
